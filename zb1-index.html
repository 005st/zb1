ZEROBASEONE (abbreviated as ZB1; Korean: 제로베이스원; RR: Jerobeiseuwon), also known as simply ZB1, is a 9-member project group formed by MNET's survival show BOYS PLANET. They are managed by WAKEONE Entertainment and their contract will last for two years and a half. They debuted on July 10, 2023, with the song IN BLOOM from their first mini album, YOUTH IN THE SHADE.
