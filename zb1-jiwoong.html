KIM JI-WOONG

Kim Ji Woong (김지웅; or simply Jiwoong), formerly known as King (킹) and Ji Nam (지남), is a South Korean singer and actor under Nest Management. He is a member of the boy group ZEROBASEONE and a former member of the boy groups ATEEN and INX. He is also a former member of the pre-debut group B.I.T.
