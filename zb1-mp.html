MELTING POINT

Melting Point is the second extended play by South Korean boy group Zerobaseone. It was released on November 6, 2023, by WakeOne and consists of five tracks, with "Crush" serving as the lead single.

The tracklist:

  • MELTING POINT
  • Take My Hand
  • CRUSH
  • Kidz Zone
  • Good Night
